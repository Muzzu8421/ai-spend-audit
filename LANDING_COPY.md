## FAQ

**Q1: Is this actually free?**
Yes. No credit card, no trial. Run the audit, see your savings, share the results — all free. We only ask for your email if you want us to send you the report.

**Q2: Do I need to connect my accounts or billing?**
No. You enter your tools and plans manually. We never access your accounts, billing dashboards, or any external systems. Just you, a form, and the results.

**Q3: How is the savings number calculated?**
We compare what you're paying against current published pricing from each vendor's official pricing page. If you're on the wrong plan for your team size, or if a cheaper tool covers your use case equally well, we flag it. Every recommendation cites a reason — not just "switch to X."

**Q4: What do you do with my email?**
We send you a copy of your audit report. If your savings are significant (>$500/month), a Credex advisor may reach out to explain how discounted AI credits work. You can opt out at any time. We don't sell your data.

**Q5: Who is Credex and why are they offering this for free?**
Credex sources discounted AI infrastructure credits — Cursor, Claude, ChatGPT Enterprise — from companies that overforecast. The audit tool helps you find where you're overspending; Credex helps you spend less on the tools you actually need. The audit is free because it's genuinely useful and because we'd rather earn your trust before asking for your business.
