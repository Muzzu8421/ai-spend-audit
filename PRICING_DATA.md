## Notes on Pricing Accuracy

- Enterprise pricing for all vendors is custom and not publicly disclosed. The audit engine uses community-reported estimates for Enterprise tiers and flags them as estimates.
- API pricing changes frequently. Prices above were verified on 2026-05-26 and may be outdated by the time of review.
- Some vendors offer annual billing discounts (10–20%). The audit engine uses monthly billing as the baseline for conservative comparison.
- Gemini availability via carrier promotions (e.g. Jio in India) is not factored into the audit engine — users are prompted to indicate if they have a promotional plan.
